1. Must extend Wallet from bitcore-client

2. Must provide Wallet method overrides for each method that requires the wallet to be unlocked. Wallet method overrides uses HOF to perform the following actions (in order)
a. Retrieve decrypted passphrase as Buffer
b. Use decrypted passphrase to call super.unlock(passphrase)
c. crypto.randomFillSync(passphrase)
d. Call super.<method being overriden>()
e. super.lock()

3. Must provide checkPassphrase(passphrase) method:
a. super.unlock(passphrase)
b. crypto.randomFillSync(passphrase)
c. super.lock()
